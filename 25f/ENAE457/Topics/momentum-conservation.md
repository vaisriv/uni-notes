- $$
    \begin{align*}
        -mv & = -(M - \Delta m) (v + \Delta v) + \Delta m (-v + u_e) \\
        & = -M \Delta v + \Delta m u_e \\
        \implies M \Delta v & = \Delta m u_e \\
        M \frac{\Delta v}{\Delta t} & = \frac{\Delta m}{\Delta t} u_e \\
        M \frac{dv}{dt} & = \dot{m} u_e
    \end{align*}
  $$

> [!IMPORTANT]
>
> ## Thrust Equation
>
> - $M a = F = \dot{m} u_e$

> [!IMPORTANT]
>
> ## Rocket Equation
>
> - $\Delta v = u_e \log(\frac{M_{1}}{M_{2}})$
